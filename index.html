Welcome to the Paradise of the North

Batanes is the northernmost and smallest province in the Philippines, known for its breathtaking rolling hills, dramatic cliffs, stone houses, and peaceful atmosphere.

Unlike the tropical scenery found in most parts of the country, Batanes offers landscapes that resemble the Scottish Highlands with vast green pastures and strong ocean waves.

It is home to the Ivatan people, a resilient community admired for their honesty, hospitality, and deep respect for nature and culture.

Discover the untouched beauty of Batanes and experience a peaceful destination filled with natural wonders, rich traditions, and unforgettable scenery.

Top Attractions

Basco Lighthouse

Located on Naidi Hills, this famous lighthouse offers a panoramic view of the sea, mountains, and the town of Basco. It is one of the most iconic tourist destinations in Batanes and a perfect spot for watching sunsets.

Valugan Boulder Beach

A breathtaking shoreline covered with giant volcanic boulders shaped by the waves of the Pacific Ocean. The peaceful atmosphere and scenic beauty make it one of the most photographed locations in Batanes.

Tukon Chapel

A peaceful stone chapel inspired by traditional Ivatan houses. Located on top of a hill, it provides breathtaking views of the landscapes and oceans surrounding Batanes.

Savidug Village

A heritage village that preserves the traditional stone houses and culture of the Ivatan people. It gives visitors a glimpse of the peaceful and historic way of life in Batanes.

Honesty Coffee Shop

A world-famous unmanned shop where customers honestly pay for what they buy without cashiers or guards, symbolizing the honesty and integrity of the Ivatan people.

Travel Guide

The fastest and most convenient way to travel to Batanes is by air. Flights are available from Manila and Clark to Basco Airport.

Estimated Airfare

Manila to Basco: ₱8,000–₱15,000

Clark to Basco: ₱10,000–₱15,000

Promo fares may go as low as ₱4,000 when booked early.

Travel Tip

Visit during March to May for better weather conditions and a smoother travel experience.
